Angela’s Cinnamon Roll Recipe

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter (melted)
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the Filling

  • ⅔ cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup butter (melted)

Instructions

  1. Mix the warm milk, yeast, sugar, egg, and egg yolk in a bowl.
  2. Add melted butter to the mixture.
  3. Add flour and mix until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough for about 10 minutes until smooth.
  5. Place in a bowl, cover, and let rise for 2 hours until doubled in size.
  6. Roll the dough into a large rectangle.
  7. Mix melted butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon for the filling.
  8. Spread filling over the dough and roll into a log.
  9. Cut into 3 cm sections and place in a baking tray.
  10. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C) and bake for 20–25 minutes until golden brown.
